About KufuMatch

KufuMatch is an Islamic marriage compatibility assessment tool designed to help Muslims find suitable life partners based on Islamic principles of Kafa'ah (compatibility). Our comprehensive questionnaire evaluates compatibility across eight key areas: Deen & Religiosity, Character & Personality, Family Values, Financial Status, Education & Career, Relationship Expectations, Lifestyle, and Important Factors.

We prioritize Islamic values with 40% weight given to religious practice and beliefs, ensuring that faith remains the foundation of every match. The assessment is completely private - no personal information is stored, only anonymous compatibility codes are used for matching.

Remember: This tool provides guidance only. Marriage is a sacred bond in Islam. Always consult your family, perform Istikhara prayer, and seek advice from knowledgeable scholars before making any decisions.
